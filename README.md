## 📊 Executive Summary
This project analyzes payment trends between traditional card payments and digital wallets (E-money). The goal is to evaluate growth patterns and understand whether digital payments are beginning to shift the financial landscape.

---

## ⚙️ Methodology
- Cleaned and processed data using Python (Pandas)
- Aggregated yearly transaction values
- Calculated growth trends
- Built Tableau dashboard for visualization

---

## 🧩 Business Problem
- Are digital wallets replacing card payments?
- Which payment method is growing faster?
- What does this mean for financial institutions?

---

## 📁 Dataset
Source: European Central Bank (ECB)
  Link:(https://www.ecb.europa.eu/stats/payment_statistics)


Includes:
- Card payments
- E-money transactions
- Credit transfers
- Total payments

---

## 🧹 Data Cleaning & Preparation
- Extracted year from dataset
- Filtered relevant payment types
- Aggregated yearly values
- Converted data into long format
- Created growth percentage (year-over-year)

## 💡 Key Insights
- Card payments dominate the market with significantly higher transaction values
- Digital wallets are growing at a faster rate
- Card payments show a decline in 2025
- Digital wallets are expanding but have not yet replaced traditional cards

---

## 📈 Growth Analysis
- E-money shows strong and consistent growth
- Card payments show volatility, including a decline in 2025

---

## 🧠 Business Recommendations
- Monitor digital wallet growth closely
- Invest in digital payment technologies
- Maintain card systems while adapting to digital trends
- Investigate the decline in card payments in 2025

---

## ⚠️ Limitations
- Based on ECB data only
- Limited geographic scope
- Does not include individual user behavior
